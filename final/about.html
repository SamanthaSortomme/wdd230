Bountiful was started by Howard H. Hinckley on 4 August 1899. His dream was to bring promise land foods to all of Zion. He attributed his good health and long life to eating Bountiful foods daily. Today his great-great-granddaughter Eliza keeps his legacy going. “If I can provide even one family with healthy choices, I know he would be proud.” Serving over a million different people every year, Bountiful keeps growing.
