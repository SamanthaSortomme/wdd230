Costco

Costco

Costco takes full advantage of proximity. Grouping like things together you can easily navigate between their featured ads, things they think you will like based on your purchases, your location, and a general search. The groups are far enough apart to see a clear separation, yet close enough together that you don't need to scroll to find them.

Godiva

Godiva

Godiva has beautiful contrast on their web page. The black and white theme contrasts with the bold gold which reflects their products as well as the elegance their brand likes to radiate. They have enough of the gold throughout that you get a warm feel on their page, but it still stands out where it is needed and highlights the box of chocolate that practically screams buy me now, but in a calm and sophisticated voice.

Robert Boyd

Robert Boyd

The white space and clean design used on Robert Boyd's website is an excellent example of how to keep a page simple. There is enough content on the page that you don't have to look for it, and the negative space provided gives a clean feel. This is a web page designed to sell art, and the categories are clearly visible to the used. This makes sure that you can find what you are looking for, or browse to your hearts delight.
