Welcome to the target audience page

Our target audience will be men and women who run a business, as well as people who have the means to buy from businesses. We will focus on people in their late 20's to late 60's as those are the people with the most spending power and most likely to start or run a shop.
We will want them to be living in Candy Land but vendors that come for show as well as visitors will be sought after too.
We will be looking for people passionate about supporting local personas
Samantha, 35, spa owner. She lives in Candy Land and runs SbubblesSpa. She offers services as well as retail. Very involved in the community and hosts free educational events for skin care. Extremely social and involved in everything local. Highschool diploma, income $60-$120,000 Likes to be with her family and play board games. Four children, a husband, three dogs and a cat. Active in all social media sites. Organization size-1. She hopes to buy a bigger home in the next 5 years. Worries about her children.

Bill, 62, close to retirement. Bill has lived in Candy Land his whole life and loves local events but misses them a lot. Organization size-200 plus.Hopes to travel more, worried about his wife's health.
